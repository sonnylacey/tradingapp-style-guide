Introduction

TradingApps allow you to build powerful, full-featured applications that will allow users to analyze, visualize, and interact with the markets in new and unique ways. These TradingApps will be hosted in TradeStation’s award winning trading platform, and exist side-by-side with in-house applications such as RadarScreen and Matrix. Whether you’re creating TradingApps for personal use, or to sell to other traders through the TradingApp store, this guide will help you to design great looking TradingApps that integrate perfectly into the TradeStation Platform.
